Turkey Club Recipe

Description

A delicious yet simple turkey club for any time of the day that anyone can make.

Ingredients

  • Any bread of choice
  • Honey mustard
  • Turkey
  • Ham
  • Lettuce
  • Swiss cheese
  • Red onion
  • Bacon

Steps

  1. Spread honey mustard on both slices of bread
  2. Place swiss cheese
  3. Add 1 layer of ham and 1 layer of turkey
  4. Place bacon
  5. Place lettuce and red onion
  6. Place top bread slice
