Steak Recipe

Description

The perfectly cooked medium rare steak.

Ingredients

  • 2 inch thick steak about the size of your hand
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Butter
  • Thyme

Steps

  1. Rub salt and pepper to all sides of the steak
  2. Let steak reach room temperature for even cooking
  3. Heat the pan and apply some butter
  4. Place steak on for 2 minutes
  5. Flip steak and place a chunk of butter with thyme in to baste
  6. Sear for 1 minute then baste steak with the melted butter for a minute
  7. Sear sides for desired crust
  8. let steak rest, while covered, for around twice as long as it took to cook the steak (4 minutes cooking steak so 8 minute rest)
